Learning Through Play

Our games are designed to be fun and educational. They help develop spatial awareness, logic, and 3D thinking skills in an engaging way.

Safe & Private

We don't need your data. High scores are stored using your browser's LocalStorage, keeping them private to your child. We use minimal, anonymous analytics to improve the site, never to track users.

Whether on a tablet, laptop, or desktop computer, our responsive design ensures a great gaming experience on any screen size.

FAQ

Do I need an account?
No. Just open a game and start playing instantly.

Where are my scores saved?
Scores are stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. This means your data stays on your device. You can clear your scores anytime from the "All Games" or "Stats" pages.

Is it really free?
Yes, 100% free. We are a passion project dedicated to providing quality educational entertainment without cost.

Can you add [X] game?
We're always open to suggestions! Use our contact form to recommend a puzzle game you'd love to see on PuzzleVerse. We focus on games that are lightweight and work well on the web.
